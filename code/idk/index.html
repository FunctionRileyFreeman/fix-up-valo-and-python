Non-Newtonian Geometry

Non-Newtonian geometry explores geometrical concepts and spaces that do not conform to the principles of classical Euclidean geometry developed by the mathematician Euclid. These non-Newtonian geometries include fractals, hyperbolic geometry, and non-Euclidean geometry, each demonstrating unique properties and deviations from traditional geometric rules.
