Raze Tips and Tricks - Valorant

1. Proper Use of Boom Bot

When using Boom Bot, try to deploy it in areas where enemies are likely to be hiding. Use it to scout ahead and gather information on enemy positions. Additionally, you can use it to force enemies out of cover or to distract them while you push.

2. Utilize Paint Shells for Area Denial

Paint Shells can be effectively used to deny areas to the enemy team. Lob them into chokepoints or common hiding spots to flush out enemies or deter them from advancing.

3. Maximizing Showstopper's Impact

Showstopper is a powerful ultimate that can clear out multiple enemies in one shot. However, timing and positioning are crucial. Try to catch enemies off guard or use it when they are grouped together to maximize its impact.
